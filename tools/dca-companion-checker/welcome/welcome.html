Getting Started

1

Install the Desktop Companion Application

If you haven't already, install DCA from your organization's software portal or the Microsoft Store.

2

Check the Extension Icon

Look for the extension icon in your browser toolbar. A green badge means DCA is running; red means it's not.

3

Open D365 Contact Center

Navigate to your D365 Contact Center workspace. You'll see a floating status indicator on the page.

4

Configure Settings (Optional)

Click the extension icon and go to settings to customize notifications, check intervals, and more.
